Comparing Declarative Animation frameworks

Feb 22, 2022
1 minute read

A evening of comparing animation in declarative frameworks. A speedrun of the animation system in Jetpack Compose as a comparison agains SwiftUI and Flutter. Hosted by GDG Nuremberg We discovered many similarites, all the systems have some way to control the animation graph, with somewhat confusing differences in naming. A tween in compose is not the same as a tween in Flutter. Flutter Tween is an animationSpec in Compose. Compose tween is a specific kind of Curve in Flutter that does a linear interpolation. Oh and its a function in Compose and an object in Flutter.
